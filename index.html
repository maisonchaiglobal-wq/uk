✦ Rift Valley · 2,000 – 2,200m

Our Rift Valley gardens sit at altitude in the Great Rift escarpment — rich volcanic soils, afternoon mist, and wide day-night temperature swings. The result is a fuller, maltier cup with deep body and a warm, rounded finish. Rift Valley black teas are exceptional for boiling with milk; the greens and purples carry an earthy intensity that lingers.

✦ Aberdares · 2,200 – 2,500m

High in the Aberdare Range above Nyeri, our mountain-garden teas benefit from cool temperatures, high rainfall, and bright equatorial light at elevation. These conditions slow leaf growth and concentrate flavour — producing a brighter, crisper, more aromatic cup with a clean, lingering sweetness. Ideal for steeping without milk; the greens and whites here are exceptional.

✦ Every sale supports WITEVA

A percentage of every sale goes directly to the Women in Tea Value Chain Association (WITEVA) — supporting training, leadership pathways, and economic inclusion for the women who grow your tea. From field to factory board, WITEVA is changing who holds power in Kenya's tea industry.
