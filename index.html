Rift Valley · 1,800 – 2,200M

Our Rift Valley gardens sit at altitude in the Great Rift escarpment, rich volcanic soils, afternoon mist, and wide day-night temperature swings. The result is a fuller, maltier cup with deep body and a warm, rounded finish. Rift Valley teas are prized for their bold character and excellent structure.

Aberdares · 2,200 – 2,500M

High in the Aberdare Range above Nyeri, our mountain-garden teas benefit from cool temperatures, high rainfall, and bright equatorial light at elevation. These conditions slow leaf growth and concentrate flavour, producing a brighter, crisper, more aromatic cup with a clean, lingering sweetness.

Every Sale Supports

Women in Tea Value Chain Association (WITEVA), a network empowering women across Kenya's tea industry, from field workers to factory board representatives. We also support Young Tea Growers, an initiative to engage young people in the tea farming business, ensuring the next generation carries Kenya's tea legacy forward.

CTC

Crush, Tear, Curl — a method producing small, dense pellets that brew quickly and release deep, robust flavour. Perfect for boiling with milk. Our Breakfast Collection is made this way.

Whole Leaf Orthodox

The traditional method, preserving the full leaf. These teas unfurl slowly, yielding layered, nuanced cups best enjoyed steeped without milk. Our Fleur, Vert, Violet, and Rare collections are crafted this way.
